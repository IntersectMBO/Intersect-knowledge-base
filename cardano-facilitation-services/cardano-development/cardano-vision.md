# Cardano 2030 Vision & Strategy

Via the process facilitated by the Cardano Product Committee of Intersect, for the first time in Cardano's history, over 700 community members collaborated to define where the ecosystem should be by 2030.

In January 2026 via [this info action](https://gov.tools/outcomes/governance_actions/3a5be93bafd26d0dbb9bfe19f4acecaa38cef75e50201d19199e6cc856db6601#0), the DReps approved the proposed Vision, Mission and Strategy framework.

The Cardano Product Committee keeps working with the community to refine and evolve the Strategy Framework and the KPIs.\
\
Intersect as an organization, and its committees, continue their work in alignment with this DRep supported direction.

You can read more in the [Product Committee Space](https://app.gitbook.com/o/Prbm1mtkwSsGWSvG1Bfd/s/K7cBOo8O1BpQ2uwlq0BE/)
