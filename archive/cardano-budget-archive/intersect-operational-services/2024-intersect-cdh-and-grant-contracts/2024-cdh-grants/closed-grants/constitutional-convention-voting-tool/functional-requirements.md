voted</td><td>M</td></tr><tr><td>11</td><td>Every constitutional delegate’s, and alternate’s profile contains the name of the workshop that elected them</td><td>As a community member I want to be able In addition to the functional requirements of the tool itself, respondents to the RFP will also need to suggest an appropriate level of user support and documentation.
