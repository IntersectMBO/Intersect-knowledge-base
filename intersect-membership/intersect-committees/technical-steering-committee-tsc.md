# Technical Steering committee (TSC)

## What is the Technical Steering Committee?

The Technical Steering Committee ensures that the governance of Cardano is based upon sound technical awareness and best practices.

## Why is this committee important?

The Technical Steering Committee provides expert technical guidance to support Cardano's development. By bringing together key developers, suppliers, and third parties, the committee advises on contract arrangements and service level agreements, creates technical proposals, and evaluates community submissions like parameter updates and hard forks, ensuring technical decisions are well-informed and sound.
