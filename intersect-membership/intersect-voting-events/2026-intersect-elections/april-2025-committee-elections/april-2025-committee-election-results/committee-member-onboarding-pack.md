# Committee Member Onboarding Pack

Welcome to the onboarding resources for newly elected committee members.

This page includes the official onboarding slide deck, created to support you as you get started in your role. It covers essential information such as your responsibilities, key documents, and the tools you’ll be using throughout your term.

Please take some time to review the presentation ahead of your onboarding call so you can come prepared and make the most of the session.
